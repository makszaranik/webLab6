Frequently Asked Questions

What are the check-in and check-out times?

Check-in: 3:00 PM, Check-out: 12:00 PM. Early check-in or late check-out may be available upon request.

Is breakfast included with my stay?

Breakfast may be included depending on your room type and rate plan. Please check your booking details.

Is Wi-Fi available at the hotel?

Yes, we provide free Wi-Fi throughout the hotel for all guests.

Are pets allowed at GoHotel?

Yes, pets are allowed in designated rooms for an additional fee. Contact us to confirm availability.

Does the hotel provide airport shuttle service?

Yes, airport shuttle service is available for an additional charge. Please contact the front desk for arrangements.

Is there parking available at the hotel?

Yes, we offer free on-site parking, subject to availability.

What is your cancellation policy?

The cancellation policy depends on your rate plan. Please refer to your booking confirmation for details.

Can I book tours or activities through the hotel?

Yes, our concierge can help you arrange local tours and activities.

Is room service available?

Yes, room service is available from 7:00 AM to 10:00 PM.

Does the hotel accommodate guests with disabilities?

Yes, we have accessible rooms and facilities. Please let us know if you need any special arrangements.
